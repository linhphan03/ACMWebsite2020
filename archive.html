Career Engagement Colloquium Takeaways 9/15/2022

Resume

  • Highlight your technical skills on your resume. For example, a listing in a separate column or check out this article.
  • Run your resume through a screen reader to make sure it recognizes the skills.
  • Include a Professional Profile on your resume, a short paragraph describing what skills you bring to the job.
  • Create LinkedIn and GitHub profiles. Include links in your resume.
  • Post some of your own projects in GitHub.
  • Consider creating a web presence as a portfolio. (Example 1, Example 2)

Networking/Job Search

  • Get on connectgettysburg.com (local linked in). Learn more here.
  • Network using LinkedIn, Handshake, Connect Gettysburg, and the Tableau Interactive Dashboard (https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/gettysburgviz/viz/OneYearOutcomesDashboard04182022/Combined ).
  • Don’t browse LinkedIn in private mode. It is ok that people know you are looking at their profile.
  • Always add a note when making a LinkedIn connection. “Hello, I’m ______. I am a student at Gettysburg College, and I would like to learn more about what you do at ________.”
  • Vary your search locations: company web sites, job sites, LinkedIn etc.
    • Handshake
    • LinkedIn
    • https://careersidekick.com/best-tech-job-boards/
    • Glassdoor – for company research

Interviewing

  • When answering interview questions remember “BAR.”
    • B: Background you have with respect to the questions.
    • A: Actions that you took.
    • R: Results of the actions you took
  • “Tell me about yourself” and “Why are you interested in this position?” are the same question. Your answer should include:
    • Why are you interested in this particular job? (couple of sentences)
    • What skills do you bring which are suitable?
    • What are your goals with respect to the position?
  • Apply for jobs for which you fit 70% or more of the requirements.
  • Glassdoor has reviews, actual interview questions, and salary data. Be careful though, reviews may be made by particularly happy or angry users.
  • Be prepared with ballpark salary data for the job and location you are interviewing for.
